About Us

Grace Noodle is a locally owned, family run, Japanese ramen noodle shop located in Shockoe Bottom. We opened in June, 2014 and specialize in fresh ramen noodles made daily in house. Every bowl of our signature noodles is painstakingly crafted one at a time and served as soon as it’s ready to ensure a fresh and authentic experience for each of our guests.

Come in and sample one of our many popular noodle bowls, including Shoyu, Gomoku, Miso, Kimchi, and Tonkotsu Ramen. We’re open Monday through Saturday, 11am to 9pm and closed every Sunday. Grace Noodle is located in the old Goree African restaurant space at 1823 East Main Street

Keep in touch with us! Follow us on online! More photos of Grace Noodle can be found on Instagram and specials can be found on Facebook, Twitter, and Google Plus. Visit our Yelp page to read or leave reviews too.
